”Being able to communicate is not the same as having language. Having language means that you are able to communicate in such a way that others understand you. Language becomes more powerful when understood by a wider community than just those closest to you. Power grows when you can communicate for more reasons to more people. The more powerful your language, the more independent you become and the more you can contribute to the community.

Language is not only a key component of communication, it is also a key aspect of identity. The words you choose to use become a part of who you are. Through language you become part of the community that speaks that language, hence the power of bilingualism. Bilingualism allows you to be part of more than one language community. The more you master a language the more powerful your connection with the community.” [Ref]

With the rapid revolution in Artificial Intelligence, AI, and Machine Learning, ML, machines become more powerful in Natural Language Processing, NLP. The next step in the revelution is to Understand languages, NLU. Humans have a pwoerful ways to describe the same thing, action or event in many different words that all have the same meaning.

In SynNet we provide a unique collection of synonyms that are the most common used in real life and how frequent each synonym used by human. We prvide a synonym for any Part of Speech type, noun, pronoun, verb, adjective, adverb, preposition, conjunction, and interjection.

Moreover, SynNet cover more than 100 languages.

How SynNet Works?

For now we suppor only file exchange, i.e. you can upload a file of any type of your select, CSV or Excel, and as a result we'll send you back a file with all the synonyms, that simple!

Please check the following three steps to start collecting data

  • Step 1: select your language. Your language must be the same as the text language from your file. We are able to detect your input language but your selection will help us to do a better job
  • Step 2: upload a file (csv, xls or xlxs) with one column of maximum of 50 words each time
  • Step 3: submit your file! and that's it

Please note that collecting the data and building your dictionary may take some time.
